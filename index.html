About the app

EasyVegan is an app that will let you search delicious and easy vegan recipes with the ingredients you already have at home. All you need to do is enter your ingredients to the list and we will do the work. You can save recipes for later, rate them or comment. You will also be a part of a community of people in the forum where you will find extra help for your recipes and your vegan diet.
